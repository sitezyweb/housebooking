Welcome to The Veranta

An Experience Beyond Stay

Nestled in the serene hills of Peermade, Kerala, The Veranta offers an unparalleled blend of luxury and nature. Our boutique homestay is designed to provide you with an authentic Kerala experience while maintaining world-class comfort.

Every corner of our property tells a story, from the traditional architecture to the modern amenities. Wake up to misty mornings, enjoy the symphony of birdsong, and immerse yourself in the tranquility that only Peermade can offer.
